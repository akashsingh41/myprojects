"A professional who is supervised is not a professional",says Subroto Bagchi in his famous book 'The Professional'. India is one of the fastest growing countries in terms of energy consumption. Currently, it is the fifth largest consumer of energy in the world, and will be the third largest by 2030.You are going to become a good engineering professional in the coming years and this country wants you, to become what it deserves to be-"THE SUPER POWER". What exactly is ‘thinking’? You must have found yourself pondering over this question. The answer is simple and sound yet sometimes difficult to grasp. If you have the power to think beyond the sky the event has lot to offer you.

Problem Statement

You have just taken the charge of Ministry of Power as a Union Minister, Government of India. Your Government has an ambitious plan of providing "POWER TO ALL" its citizens by year 2015 .For this mission, India needs an installed generation capacity of at least 230,000 MW by 2015 against the present installed capacity of 165000 MW. As someone very passionate about this idea, you want to exploit all the possibilities to make this mission realize. Suggest a comprehensive plan to realize this mission.
Concentrate your solution not only on generation, but also on reduction of losses and energy conservation. Also keep in mind that the conventional resources are limited and the present power generation is mainly based upon thermal energy(coal based), so think of non conventional sources for electricity generation.

The present installed generation capacity from different sources is given as follows :-
-Thermal-106,000 MW
-Hydro-34,000 MW
-Nuclear-4560 MW
-Renewable-17,000 MW.


You may also think on the following Ideas :-
1. Recycled Energy
http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Energy_recycling

2. Smart Grid
http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Smart_grid

3. Renewable Energy(Geothermal and ocean energy being of prime importance)

http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Geothermal_energy
http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Marine_energy

4. Urban waste management.

5. Energy conservation.

6. Reducing Transmission and distribution losses.

7. ANY NEW INNOVATIVE IDEA FROM YOUR SIDE



NOTE- You are the new generation and this environment also needs some attention of yours.


Rules and regulations

1. Team of maximum three members.

2. The competition will be in three stages:-

    a) Abstract submission in MS Word 97 format.The abstract should contain all the details of your plan.

     (b) On the bases of abstracts 6-7 teams would be shortlisted to compete in the presentation round.Here the teams would present there idea to the judges in Power Point Presentation for 12 minutes.

     (c) 4-5 teams would then advance to the Group Discussion round.Here the teams would fight among themselves to bring out the flaws in opponents.

3. Judges decision shall be treated as final and binding .

4. The last date of submission is 18th January 2011 5.00pm.

5. You can submit the abstract to the event coordinators:
