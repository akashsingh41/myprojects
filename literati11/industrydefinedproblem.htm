"Scientists investigate that which already is;
Engineers create that which has never been."

-Albert Einstein

At IDP, we give you the perfect podium to showcase that Einstein inside you, where you exhibit your engineering skills using the power of your grey cells, a challenge to innovate and find solutions.

This year, at Literati 2011 put your imagination at work as the event is getting its first major facelift, much bigger than all these years. Focus is on the future, the uncertain tomorrow. The problem statement will be more diverse, inter-disciplined, seeking solutions that quench the pursuit for the skyrocketing demands of mankind. So turn that searchlight inwards and be ready to discover the real engineer in you.

Event Format
This event will be held in two phases.

Round 1:
Participating teams should send their write ups before the respective deadlines.

Round 2:
1. Teams will be shortlisted based on their report.
2. The shortlisted teams will have to make a 10 minute presentation of their ideas during Literati’11.
3. Other details will be sent to the selected teams later.

Rules and Regulation
1. A Maximum of three members per team is allowed.
2. Multiple submission is allowed (Either of the 2 problems or both)
3. The deadline for submission of entries for both the problem statements has been kept
     till 17th January, 2011.
4. The decision of the judges will be final and binding.
