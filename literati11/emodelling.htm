Defy all limits. Think beyond, bend the known dimensions and step into a whole new world. Interweave your skill into the magic of electronic designing. The perfect platform for emerging hardware professionals. An on the spot designing competition, it draws innovative talent countrywide. Improvisation skills and the finer points of digital and analog electronics to be tested on real time situations.

Problem Statement to be uploaded soon...


The event constitutes three rounds and there will be a team of two.

Round 1: Theoretical question paper (objective/subjective) separate for first/second year and pre-final year.

Round 2: Round 2 comprises two phases

  Phase 1: On projector quiz (separate for first/second year and pre-final year)
  Phase 2: One designing problem(on paper) followed by a discussion.

Round 3: In this round you may have to design either a circuit on orcad or realize some analog/digital communication problem on communication lab.

Round 3 will be followed by a Viva from a panel of teachers.

Note: There will be an initial problem statement, on the submission of it’s solution on time will privilege you for a wild card entry direct in second round.
