  • Event Description
  • Rules & Regulations
  • Event Coordinator

You have always been fascinated by the world of business. You love hearing business leaders speak. You get amazingly motivated to learn success stories of big companies. You jump out of your seat when you hear staggering revenue and profit figures of large corporate. Your face lights up when you observe an amazingly new business model.

If you have similar experiences as the ones described above, chances are that you may be genuinely interested in starting up your business. You also may have a lot of ideas in your mind, but all of those are still inchoate. So, let your ideas keep rolling and let your grey cells be pushed harder, as the time has finally come to show up your real business acumen. The stage is all set. Go Next. Now



General Instructions

1. The B plan being proposed should either be based on a new and innovative business model or should be uniquely positioned to challenge the present market set up.
2. The idea /plan should be the team’s own creation and not a copy of already existing plan.
3. The team shall contain a maximum of 4 members.
4. One student can participate in one team only.
5. The decisions by event coordinators will be final and binding.

Round 1-submission of executive summary

1. An executive summary of not more than 2000 words is to be submitted which must necessarily include:
a. The Idea and its USP
b. Analysis of the Market – competitors, competitive edges, target market, marketing strategy
c. Financial Details –Initial set up cost, break even analysis
d. Infrastructure and workforce requirements
e. The Exit Plan

2. All entries are to be mailed to aceventura.literati11@gmail.com, with subject 'Ace Ventura'. The teams are also required to submit a hard copy of the same folded into an envelope in Hostel-8, room no. 233(for boys) and Kaveri hostel, A-block, room no. 305(for girls). The entries should include contact number of the team members. (On the basis of the executive summaries, the top 14 teams will be short listed for round 2)
3. Last date for submission of executive summaries is 20th January, 2011,2359 hrs.

Round 2-Discussion with coordinators

The short listed candidates/teams will have to discuss their idea in detail with the coordinators. (The discussion will include all the requisite details like the mode of implementation, the business potential and etc.). After this round, around 8 teams will be selected for the next round.


Final Round- Presentation of business plan

1. The presentation should be in power point ( *.ppt) format.
2. Each team will be given 10 minutes to present their plan followed by a 2 minute interaction with judges and the audience.
3. Teams are also required to prepare a detailed report in a maximum of 4000 words.
(Format specifications: Times New Roman, Size 12, single line spacing. The entries must be in *.doc/.docx format)
