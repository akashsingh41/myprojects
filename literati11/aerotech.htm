Having interest in the world of airplanes and the way they fly??
LITERATI ’11 presents "A technical paper presentation for all those who are interested in field related to aeronautical sciences."


Suggested Topics

1. Concept Model (Aeromodel or Aircraft). ***
2. Alternate Propulsion techniques.
3. Flying Cars.
4. Radiation exposure on commercial flights.
5. Variable Specific Impulse Magnetoplasma Rocket (VASIMR).
6. Landing and take-off of aerospace vehicle.
7. Boundary Layer Effects in Aerodynamics
8. Choppers technology in future.
9. Hovering Vehicles.
10. Advanced fuels for aerospace vehicle.

Students can also present topics related to aeronautical sciences other than these.

1. The short listing shall be done on the basis of an abstract of the topic to be presented.

2. Abstract should be limited to 300 words and it should give an appropriate idea of what the topic of presentation is all about and what shall be the frame of the presentation.

3. The abstract should be mailed to aeromodelling.nitk@gmail.com latest by 23:59 hrs, Jan’22, 2011.

4. The subject of the mail is to be kept as “Abstract for Aerotech”.

5. The selected entries shall be informed within a day or two after the final day of submission and thereafter a detailed report of the presentation in IEEE format has to be mailed to the same ID latest by Jan 25, 2011.

6. The number of pages must not exceed 10 including the cover page.

7. Three hard copies of the report should also be submitted to the coordinators 3 hrs before the presentation.
