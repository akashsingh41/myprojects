Math-o-logica


"Pure mathematics is the world's best game. It is more
 absorbing than chess, more of a gamble than poker, and
 lasts longer than Monopoly.  It's free.  It can be
 played anywhere" 
                                         -Richard J. Trudeau

Here we bring to you this game : a tantalizing and compelling pursuit of mathematical problems which offers mental absorption and endless challenges.


It is an excellent opportunity to exercise your grey cells and test your wit and ingenuity to straighten the toughest tangles. Make sure to board this train threading the landscape of numbers taking refuge from the goading urgency of contingent happenings , into a land of excitement , adventure and sheer delight.

9. Clearly mention your Name, College name and Contact details with the solution set.
