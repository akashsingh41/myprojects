About Kurukshetra

Kurukshetra, the land of pantheons, the ancient land where Gods, men, paladins, battled death and conquered their fears for their cause. On this very land is the foundation of NIT Kurukshetra. The institute, like most of its ilk setup for the task to which now has attained its NIT status, has fulfilled its quest towards its goals. It is one of the premiere institutes which will steer the country in the coming years. The institute unit in its true sense portrays the thought of national integration. It is a vast conglomerate of all that is Indian. Student from all over the country thrive in harmony. And what really breeds through in being are the involvement and the enthusiasm of the students led by a capable and dedicated faculty. The students here excel on cultural and artistic fields also. The plethora of shows organized here bears witness to it.



About NIT Kurukshetra

For eons, the Karmic philosophy has inspired and revolutionized the lives of millions. The soil of Kurukshetra, which bore testimony to the divine revelation of the Gita by Krishna to his beloved Partha, continues to create history, in a different way though. Set across the dusty ochre landscape of the northern plains, National Institute of Technology, Kurukshetra is one of the premeire technical institutes of the country. A long illustrious history and hugely successful alumni have helped NITK to carve out a niche for itself. The various hues of the Indian culture amalgamate here to form the timeless masterpiece called “India”. The diversity of the land and hence, the inherent unity that form a part of the NITK life, truly justify, why we call ourselves The National Institute of Technology. With a world class faculty and dedicated students, the quest for excellence is perennial. And as the old adage goes, where there is a will, there is a way.

About Technobyte

Technobyte is a sophisticated and professional outfit of the institute where the brains of the COMPUTER ENGINEERING and INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY masterminds are nurtured and the talent of the whiz kids synchronized to make every programme successful. In recent times, Technobyte has been a hub of activities and have brought laurels to the Institution. To call them merely a society would undermine their very value. Here all the students get integrated and their confidence is reinforced, so as to drive greater mileage from them. Primary focus is on the technical aspects of their branch, which touches every nook and corner of the vast info highway. We are not only a technical society but also an object oriented one where every single viewpoint is paid heed to. We strive to achieve perfection par excellence with several successful events under our belt. Technobyte has been organizing IRIS , the National Level Technical symposiums of NIT Kurukshetra for more than a decade. The events have been witnessing increased response from institutions throughout the country. They have been an unbelievable opera of endowment, skills and talent. Keeping in view the ever increasing popularity and exuberance of to-be-engineers, the society as decided to conduct another episode of IRIS, "Iris '09" , and is expected to make a glorious breakthrough.
